About Us

At Stroke Predict, we are a team of dedicated data scientists, healthcare professionals, and web developers committed to making health insights accessible. Our mission is to utilize cutting-edge machine learning technology to assist individuals and healthcare providers in understanding stroke risk factors more effectively.

We believe that informed decisions lead to better health outcomes. Our model is trained on a comprehensive dataset of health metrics to ensure accuracy and reliability in its predictions.
